About Me

Who Am I?

Hello! I'm David Roy, a 17-year-old creative college student with a passion for design and languages. My mission is to transform your ideas into stunning visuals and bridge communication gaps through precise translations.

As a designer, I craft eye-catching graphics, including logos, social media posts, greeting cards, invitations, and complete brand identities. My work is tailored to make your brand stand out with a modern, professional touch.

As a translator, I'm fluent in English, Bangla, and Hindi, offering seamless translations in any direction between these languages. Whether it's business documents or creative content, I ensure your message resonates across cultures.
